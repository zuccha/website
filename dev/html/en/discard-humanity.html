Cards Against Humanity

Cards Against Humanity is a card game. A black card common to all players is drawn from the deck, and it features a sentence with one or more blank spaces. Each player must choose a white card from its hand to fill those blank spaces. Once everyone has chosen the card, the player with the funniest (and most politically incorrect) combination is chosen as winner. Then a new turn starts and a new black card is drawn.

Discard Humanity

Discard Humanity has been developed by Lara Bruseghini, Valerie Burgener, Renzo Cotti, Aron Fiechter and me as a project for the Web Atelier course from USI.

Features

  • Deck selection and creation
  • Private and public games
  • Winning condition selection
  • Czar or democracy voting mode
