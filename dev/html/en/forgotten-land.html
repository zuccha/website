The Forgotten Land

The Forgotten Land is an isometric strategy game developed by me, Samuele Bischof, Valerio Battaglia, Alexander Fischer and Francesco Sani during our first year at USI. The game has been developed with Java and libGDX. The purpose of the game is to build houses, balance resources and make your civilisation last as long as possible.
