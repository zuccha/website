The Jam

A game jam is an event where people meet to create games from scratch in a limited amount of time. During the 2017 Global Game Jam, we had 48 hours to create a game with the theme waves. The Global Game Jam was hosted in different locations worldwide. I participated at the event organized by Steelix Software at SUPSI in Manno, Switzerland.

The Team

I worked along with Enrico Bottani, Luca Menghini and Daniel Ineichen. Enrico and I did the programming, Luca worked on graphics and Daniel composed the music. I never met them before the jam, but it has been a pleasure for me to work with and learn from them.

The Game

The game is nothing but a simple shooter: enemies come from above and the player must destroy them with sound waves (blue for blue enemies, yellow for brown ones).

However, the distinctive trait of the game is commands are captured through the microphone. Low volume sounds will trigger blue projectiles, high volume sounds will shoot yellow waves and continued sounds will activate a protective shield. It's up to the player find the right balance.

The Development

The game has been developed using Unity. We started working on the project on Friday night, brainstorming ideas, but it wasn't until Saturday we actually knew what we were going to do. Some of us even slept at SUPSI in order to keep working. We finally completed and submitted the game on Sunday evening.

Since we had a limited amount of time to make it, the game is not perfect. We advise to use headphones to enjoy the music or to play with the speakers on mute, otherwise the game will play by itself!
